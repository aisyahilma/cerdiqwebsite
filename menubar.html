Welcome to Pesantren Cerdiq

Pesantren Cerdiq is an educational institution dedicated to providing a balanced education combining religious and academic knowledge. Our mission is to nurture the next generation of leaders who are not only knowledgeable but also ethical and compassionate.

About Us

Established in 1995, Pesantren Cerdiq has grown to become a leading institution in the region, known for its commitment to excellence in education and character building. Our dedicated team of educators and staff work tirelessly to create an environment conducive to learning and personal growth.
