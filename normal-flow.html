Welcome to Cerdiq

Cerdiq is a place of learning and spiritual growth. We offer a comprehensive curriculum that combines religious studies with modern education. Our mission is to nurture knowledgeable, pious, and socially responsible individuals.

Our Vision

We envision a community where individuals are empowered with the knowledge and values to contribute positively to society. Our pesantren strives to produce graduates who are not only well-versed in Islamic teachings but also equipped with the skills to excel in the contemporary world.
