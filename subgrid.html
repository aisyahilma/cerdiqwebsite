Additional Info

At Pesantren Cerdiq, our mission is to provide a balanced education that integrates traditional Islamic values with modern academic knowledge. Our goal is to produce students who are both intellectually competent and spiritually grounded.

Our curriculum is designed to foster critical thinking and ethical behavior, ensuring that students are well-prepared for the challenges of the future. We offer a wide range of subjects that cater to the diverse interests and talents of our students.

The facilities at Pesantren Cerdiq are state-of-the-art, including fully equipped classrooms, a comprehensive library, and extensive sports facilities. We aim to create a nurturing and stimulating environment for all our students.

Community engagement is a core value at Pesantren Cerdiq. We encourage our students to participate in various social and community service activities, fostering a sense of responsibility and empathy towards others.

For more information, visit www.pesantrencerdiq.id or contact us at info@pesantrencerdiq.id
