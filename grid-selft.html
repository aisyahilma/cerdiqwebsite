Additional Info

Pesantren Cerdiq is dedicated to providing a balanced education that harmonizes traditional Islamic teachings with modern academic knowledge. Our aim is to nurture students who are knowledgeable, ethical, and prepared for the challenges of the future.

The curriculum at Pesantren Cerdiq is carefully designed to cover a broad spectrum of subjects. Students are encouraged to develop their intellectual capabilities while also strengthening their faith and moral values.

Our facilities are designed to support a conducive learning environment. We offer well-equipped classrooms, a comprehensive library, and various sports facilities to ensure our students' holistic development.

Pesantren Cerdiq also emphasizes community engagement, encouraging students to participate in various social activities that promote a sense of responsibility and empathy.

For more information, visit www.pesantrencerdiq.id or contact us at info@pesantrencerdiq.id
