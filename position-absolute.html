Welcome to Pesantren Cerdiq

Empowering Students with Knowledge and Faith

Pesantren Cerdiq is committed to providing a comprehensive educational experience that combines traditional Islamic teachings with modern academic curriculum. Our mission is to nurture students who are not only knowledgeable but also morally upright and socially responsible.

Our curriculum emphasizes critical thinking, creativity, and ethical behavior, preparing students to excel in a globalized world. We offer a wide range of subjects and extracurricular activities to cater to the diverse interests and talents of our students.

Located in a serene environment conducive to learning, Pesantren Cerdiq provides state-of-the-art facilities including classrooms equipped with the latest technology, a well-stocked library, and recreational amenities. We prioritize holistic development, ensuring our students thrive academically, spiritually, and emotionally.

Join us at Pesantren Cerdiq and embark on a journey of knowledge, growth, and community service. Together, we strive to make a positive impact on society through education and empowerment.
