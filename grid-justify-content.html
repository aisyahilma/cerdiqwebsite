Additional Info

Pesantren Cerdiq is a modern educational institution that combines traditional Islamic teachings with contemporary academic excellence. Our mission is to cultivate students who are not only academically proficient but also possess strong moral and spiritual values.

At Pesantren Cerdiq, students engage in a rigorous academic program alongside a comprehensive religious curriculum. This dual approach ensures that our students are well-prepared for both higher education and their roles as ethical leaders in society.

Our facilities include state-of-the-art classrooms, a well-stocked library, and various recreational areas that support a balanced lifestyle. We also offer numerous extracurricular activities that promote personal growth and community engagement.

For more information, visit www.pesantrencerdiq.id or contact us at info@pesantrencerdiq.id
